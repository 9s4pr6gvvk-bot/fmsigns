Important Legal Notice — Please Read Before Ordering

FM Signs does not condone the unlawful placement of signage on public roads, public land, or land for which you do not have permission. This product is designed and sold for use on private land only. It is the sole responsibility of the purchaser to ensure lawful placement.

The placement of signs on public highways in the United Kingdom is governed by strict legislation, including:

  • The Traffic Signs Regulations and General Directions 2016 (TSRGD)
  • The Highways Act 1980
  • The Town and Country Planning Act 1990 (advertisement consent)

Unauthorised signs on public roads or verges may be illegal and subject to removal by the local authority or Highways England. Placing signs without appropriate consent could result in fines or enforcement action.

Suitable locations for this sign include private farm tracks, private driveways, stable yards, paddocks, and any land where the landowner's express written permission has been obtained. If you are unsure whether placement is lawful, please consult your local council or a qualified legal adviser before purchasing.

FM Signs accepts no liability for signage placed unlawfully or without appropriate authority.
